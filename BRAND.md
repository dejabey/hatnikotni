# Hatnikotni

## Company Name

Hatnikotni

---

## Meaning

Hatnikotni is derived from two Northern Malay dialect words.

- Hatni — "this one" / "these"
- Kotni — "this way" / "towards here"

Together, the name represents the idea of guiding people towards something useful, meaningful and worth discovering.

---

## Positioning

Hatnikotni is an independent digital studio that creates thoughtful digital products.

Rather than building solutions on demand, Hatnikotni focuses on developing its own ideas into practical and useful digital experiences.

---

## What We Build

- Independent digital products
- Web-based platforms
- Community-focused projects
- Long-term ideas with practical value

---

## Mission

To create independent digital products that are simple, useful and built to last.

Rather than chasing trends, Hatnikotni focuses on solving real problems through thoughtful digital experiences.

---

## Principles

### Build with Purpose

Every project should solve a real problem.

### Keep It Simple

Simple products are easier to understand, maintain and improve.

### Quality over Quantity

A few well-crafted projects are more valuable than many unfinished ideas.

### Think Long Term

Projects should remain useful for years, not just for launch day.

---

## What Hatnikotni Is

Hatnikotni is an independent digital studio focused on creating its own products.

The studio exists to turn thoughtful ideas into practical digital experiences that people can genuinely use and benefit from.

Projects are built because they are worth building, not because they are commissioned.

---

## What Hatnikotni Isn't

Hatnikotni is not a traditional web agency.

It does not actively offer custom web development, digital marketing or software services for hire.

Any collaboration or partnership is considered selectively and only when it aligns with the studio's values and long-term direction.

---

## Brand Voice

Hatnikotni communicates in a calm, thoughtful and honest manner.

The studio avoids exaggerated marketing language, empty promises and unnecessary technical jargon.

Every message should be clear, practical and easy to understand.

Instead of trying to impress people, Hatnikotni aims to earn trust through transparency, consistency and meaningful work.
